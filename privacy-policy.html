Overview

ChessConnect ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it — in plain language.

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of ChessConnect, including chess coaches who register on our platform and students or parents who browse coach profiles. By using ChessConnect, you agree to the terms of this policy.

We operate in compliance with India's Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023. ChessConnect acts as a "Data Fiduciary" under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, and is responsible for determining the purpose and means of processing your personal data.

ChessConnect is a directory platform connecting independent coaches and students. We do not supervise, control, or guarantee the services provided by listed coaches.

Lawful Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on your explicit consent given at the time of registration. By submitting the registration form, you consent to the collection and use of your data as described in this policy.

You may withdraw this consent at any time by contacting us at support@chessconnect.online. Withdrawing consent will result in your profile being deactivated and your data being scheduled for deletion.

Data We Collect

From Coaches (Registration)

  • Full name — for your public profile
  • WhatsApp number — for verification and direct student contact
  • City — for location-based discovery
  • Coaching details — mode, experience, levels taught, languages
  • FIDE ID and rating — if applicable, for credibility badge
  • Profile photo — optional, displayed on your public profile
  • Bio and fees — displayed on your public profile

From All Visitors (Automatically)

  • Browser type and device — for compatibility
  • Pages visited and time spent — for improving the platform
  • IP address — for security and abuse prevention

Cookies & Analytics

We may use essential cookies and analytics tools to improve website performance and understand how visitors use our platform. These do not collect sensitive personal data. If we introduce third-party analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly.

⚠️ We do not collect payment information. ChessConnect is currently free for coaches. All fee transactions happen directly between coaches and students — we are not involved.

How We Use Your Data

We use your data only for the purposes listed below:

  • To create and display your public coach profile
  • To verify your identity before activating your profile
  • To contact you on WhatsApp for profile verification or platform updates
  • To prevent spam, fake profiles, and abuse on the platform
  • To improve ChessConnect's features and user experience
  • To comply with legal obligations under Indian law

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or sell it to any third party.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database platform with servers located outside India (currently in the Asia-Pacific region). By using ChessConnect, you consent to this cross-border data transfer.

We retain your data for as long as your profile is active on ChessConnect. If you request deletion, we will remove your data within 30 days. Please note that backup copies of data may persist for up to 60 additional days before permanent deletion from all systems.

Data Sharing

Your public profile data (name, city, coaching details, bio, photo, fees) is visible to anyone who visits ChessConnect. Your WhatsApp number is also displayed publicly on your approved profile so students can contact you directly.

⚠️ By submitting your WhatsApp number during registration, you expressly consent to its public display on your coach profile for the purpose of enabling direct student contact. If you wish to remove it at any time, contact us and we will deactivate your profile promptly.

We do not share your data with any third-party companies, advertisers, or data brokers. We may share data with law enforcement if required by Indian law.

Your Rights (DPDP Act 2023)

Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023, you have the following rights:

  • Right to access — Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Right to correction — Ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — Request deletion of your data and profile
  • Right to withdraw consent — You may withdraw consent at any time, which will result in your profile being deactivated
  • Right to grievance redressal — Raise a complaint with us directly

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@chessconnect.online. We will respond within 72 hours.

In accordance with the DPDP Act, 2023, if your complaint remains unresolved after contacting us, you may escalate it to the Data Protection Board of India once it is constituted and operational.

Children's Privacy

ChessConnect does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 18 without verifiable parental consent. Coach registrations are for adults only. If you believe a minor has submitted data without consent, please contact us immediately.

Security

We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data, including:

  • Row Level Security (RLS) on our database — profiles are not publicly visible until manually approved by our team
  • HTTPS encryption on all data transmissions
  • No profile goes live without manual verification by the ChessConnect team

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but are committed to best practices.

Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. If changes are significant, we will notify registered coaches via WhatsApp.

As ChessConnect evolves and introduces new features — such as payments, student accounts, subscriptions, or analytics tools — this policy may be expanded to reflect additional data practices. We will always notify you of material changes before they take effect.
